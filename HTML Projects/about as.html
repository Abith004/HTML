About as


NDTV (New Delhi Television Limited) is a prominent Indian news network established in 1988, renowned for its comprehensive coverage across multiple channels including NDTV 24x7, NDTV India, and NDTV Profit. Led by founders Radhika Roy and Prannoy Roy, the network excels in delivering news, current affairs, and analysis spanning politics, business, sports, and entertainment.
